🔒 Your Data, Your Choice

SnapBit provides multiple ways for you to delete your data. You can:

  • Delete specific data (habits and logs) while keeping your account
  • Delete your entire account and all associated data permanently
  • Request deletion via email if you prefer human assistance

All deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

Option 1: Delete Data Without Deleting Account

If you want to clear all your habits and tracking data but keep your SnapBit account, follow these steps:

Open SnapBit App

Launch the SnapBit app on your device and ensure you are signed in.

Go to Settings

Tap on the Settings tab at the bottom navigation bar (gear icon).

Scroll to "About" Section

Scroll down to find the About section near the bottom of the settings page.

Tap "Delete All Data"

Tap on "Delete All Data" (shown in orange text).

Confirm Deletion

A confirmation dialog will appear. Tap "Delete" to confirm. This will permanently delete all your habits, logs, and photos stored in the app.

What gets deleted: All habits, completion logs, streak data, and photos stored within the app. Your account and preferences will remain intact.

Option 2: Delete Your Account Completely

To permanently delete your SnapBit account and all associated data, follow these steps:

Open SnapBit App

Launch the SnapBit app on your device and ensure you are signed in.

Go to Settings

Tap on the Settings tab at the bottom navigation bar (gear icon).

Scroll to "About" Section

Scroll down to find the About section near the bottom of the settings page.

Tap "Delete Account"

Tap on "Delete Account" (shown in red text).

Re-authenticate (if required)

For security, you may be asked to sign in with Google again to confirm your identity.

Confirm Account Deletion

A confirmation dialog will appear. Tap "Delete Account" to permanently delete your account.

⚠ Warning: This Action is Irreversible

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

  • Your user profile and account information
  • All habits and tracking data
  • All photos stored within the app
  • Streak history and progress analytics
  • Any premium subscription status (manage through Google Play)

This cannot be undone. Make sure you want to proceed before confirming.

Option 3: Request Data Deletion via Email

If you prefer to request data deletion through email, or if you're unable to access your account, you can contact us directly:

Send an Email

Send an email to inovasitiglobal@gmail.com with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request - SnapBit"

Include Your Information

In your email, please include:

  • The email address associated with your SnapBit account
  • Whether you want to delete all data only or your entire account
  • A brief confirmation that you understand this action is permanent

Wait for Confirmation

We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Hi SnapBit Team,

What Data Does SnapBit Collect?

Here's a complete overview of the data SnapBit collects and what happens when you delete it:

Data Type Description Storage Location
Account Info Email, display name, profile photo (from Google) Firebase Authentication
Habits Habit names, categories, reminder times, icons Local device database
Completion Logs Daily check-ins, streak data, timestamps Local device database
Photos Habit proof photos you capture Local device storage
Settings Notification preferences, camera settings Local device storage
Analytics Anonymous usage data, crash reports Firebase Analytics

Privacy Note: Your habit data and photos are stored locally on your device by default and are never uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly share them. We prioritize your privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to delete my data? ▼

When you delete data through the app, it is removed immediately from your device. If you request deletion via email, we process requests within 30 days and will send you a confirmation once complete.

Can I recover my data after deletion? ▼

No. Once data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. We recommend exporting any important information before proceeding with deletion. If you saved photos to your device's gallery (Premium feature), those will remain on your device.

What happens to my premium subscription if I delete my account? ▼

Deleting your SnapBit account does not automatically cancel your Google Play subscription. You must cancel your subscription separately through the Google Play Store to avoid future charges. Go to Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > SnapBit > Cancel.

Will my photos be deleted if I only delete my data (not account)? ▼

Yes, choosing "Delete All Data" will delete all photos stored within the SnapBit app. However, if you have the "Photo Gallery" option enabled (Premium), copies saved to your device's gallery will remain.

I can't access my account. How can I delete my data? ▼

If you cannot access your account, please send a data deletion request via email to inovasitiglobal@gmail.com. Include the email address associated with your account, and we will verify your identity and process your request.

Does SnapBit share my data with third parties? ▼

No. SnapBit does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We use Firebase for authentication and analytics, and Google AdMob for ads (free tier only). For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

Can I delete individual habits instead of all data? ▼

Yes! You can delete individual habits from the Habits tab. Tap on a habit, then select "Delete Habit" from the options. This will remove that specific habit and its associated logs and photos.

How do I contact support from within the app? ▼

Go to Settings > Help & Support > Contact Support / Feedback. This will open your email app with a pre-filled template that includes your device information, making it easier for us to help you.

How long does it take to get a response to my support request? ▼

We typically respond to all support requests within 1-2 business days. For more complex issues, it may take a bit longer, but we'll always acknowledge your request promptly.

What information should I include when reporting a bug? ▼

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include: (1) Your device model and OS version, (2) SnapBit app version, (3) A clear description of the problem, (4) Steps to reproduce the issue, and (5) Any error messages you saw. Using the in-app feedback feature automatically includes device info!

Can I request a new feature? ▼

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Send your feature requests to inovasitiglobal@gmail.com with "Feature Request" in the subject line. While we can't implement every suggestion, we carefully consider all feedback when planning updates.

I'm having issues with my Premium subscription. Who do I contact? ▼

For subscription issues: (1) First, check your subscription status in Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions. (2) For billing disputes or refund requests, contact Google Play Support directly as they manage all payments. (3) For app-related subscription features not working, contact us at inovasitiglobal@gmail.com.
